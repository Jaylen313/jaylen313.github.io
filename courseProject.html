Course Projects

Here are projects I’ve worked on, what they do, the tools used, and my role.

Much To Do (Godot / GDScript)

MuchToDo is a to-do list first and a game second. Users complete real-life tasks to gain XP and level up their character. Higher level = more damage to enemies. After defeating enough enemies, the Area Boss can be challenged.

Key Features

  • Add custom tasks/goals
  • Mark tasks complete → grants XP
  • Attack damage scales with character level
  • Boss challenges + stage progression
  • Multiple screens/menus (main, list, add/edit, hero, stage select)

Tools + My Role

  • Tools: Godot Engine, GDScript
  • My role: Backend: Task + goal management system (creating, organizing, updating)

Lord of Space (JavaFX)

A JavaFX space shooter. You move the ship, shoot enemies, avoid hits and meteors, and try to earn a high score.

Features

  • Multiple enemy types
  • Explosions + shooting effects
  • Score tracking
  • Simple controls
  • Animated background for “flying through space” feel
  • Controls: Mouse move (horizontal), left click to shoot

Tools + My Role

  • Tools: Java, JavaFX
  • My role: Full creator

SmartFood AI (Full-Stack)

A kitchen + health companion app: manage pantry inventory, scan receipts, detect duplicates, look up nutrition and barcodes, and get AI-powered recipe suggestions based on what you already have.

Core Features

  • Smart pantry management + expiration alerts
  • Receipt scanner (OCR + LLM parsing)
  • Duplicate detection for receipts/items
  • Barcode + nutrition lookups
  • AI recipe suggestions based on pantry
  • Household sharing + expense tracking

Tech Stack + My Role

  • Backend: Node.js, Express, Supabase (Postgres + RLS), JWT
  • APIs: Google Cloud Vision, OpenRouter (GPT-4), Edamam, Barcode lookup
  • Caching: Upstash Redis
  • Frontend: Pure HTML/CSS/JS (dark theme)
  • My role: Backend
